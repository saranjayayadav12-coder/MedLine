About MediLine AI

Advanced Healthcare Platform · Version 2.0

MediLine AI

MediLine AI is a next-generation healthcare companion that connects patients with top specialists, provides AI-driven symptom analysis, prescription scanning, and seamless appointment booking — all in one unified platform.

Key Features
AI Symptom Analysis
Describe your symptoms in plain language and get an instant AI-powered health assessment with specialist routing.
Document & Rx Scanner
Upload prescriptions, lab reports, or medical documents (Image/PDF/Text) for instant AI analysis and medicine breakdown.
Smart Appointment Booking
Browse top-rated specialists by category, read patient reviews, and book appointments with time-slot selection.
Video Consultations
Start secure video calls directly from any doctor's card for remote consultations without leaving the app.
Multilingual Support
Communicate in English, Hindi, or Bengali. MediBot auto-detects your language and responds accordingly.
Voice Input
Speak your symptoms using the built-in voice recorder. Supports voice input in multiple languages via Web Speech API.
Health Dashboard
Track your health score, appointment history, weekly activity, and get personalized daily health tips.
Privacy First
All data stored locally in your browser. No third-party data sharing. Your health information stays private.
How It Works
1
Sign Up & Set Up Your Profile
Create a free account and fill in your health details — blood group, DOB, gender — for a personalised experience.
2
Describe Your Symptoms or Upload a Document
Chat with MediBot AI, use voice input, or upload a prescription / lab report for instant analysis.
3
Get Smart Specialist Routing
The AI assesses your condition, assigns a priority level, and recommends the right specialist for you.
4
Book an Appointment or Start a Video Call
Pick a doctor, select a date & time slot, confirm your booking, or start an instant video consultation.
